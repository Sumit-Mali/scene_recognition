About Project

Human Brain knows how to recognize things by visually looking at them. But, the question is about the sufficiently trained artificial system which matches the human ability to classify the scene in an image or video is still an open quest. So to overcome with this solution we have developed model which can recognize the scene from photos, videos, or real time camera feed.
Scene recognition plays a crucial role in computer vision application. It can be used for various applications ranging from autonomous vehicles to surveillance systems. The ability to identify and understand scene is essential for enhancing the capabilities of various technologies. Scene recognition is a fundamental task in computer vision, involving the categorization of images or videos into predefined classes based on their visual content.
In this project, we address the scene recognition challenge by using deep learning techniques, specifically transfer learning with the ResNet50 architecture. We are using Intel Image Classification Dataset comprising images from five distinct scene categories such as Forest, Sea, Mountain, Glacier and Street.
